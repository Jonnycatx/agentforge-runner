User Flow (Correct Way)
Step	What Happens
1	New user visits AgentForge web app
2	Builds their agent (name, personality, tools, etc.)
3	Clicks "Deploy to Desktop"
4	First time only: Downloads Runner app from YOUR central repo
5	Downloads their .agentforge file (tiny config)
6	Double-clicks .agentforge → Runner opens with their agent
7	Next time: Just downloads new .agentforge files (Runner already installed)
